What is SEO backlinking, and why is it important for my channel?

SEO backlinking involves creating links from other websites back to your YouTube channel. These backlinks act as endorsements, signaling to search engines that your content is valuable and relevant. This can boost your channel’s authority, improve its search rankings, and drive more organic traffic to your videos.
